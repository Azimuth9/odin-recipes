How to Eat a Mango



There are different ways in which a person can approach eating a mango. One may devour
the fruit with great force, peeling it's skin off with their razor sharp teeth; or one may
choose to stealthy approach the operation, softly sucking on mango's crown, and then going
and slicing its body with surgery precision, and once dismembered, picking the inners off
with an instrument.




"Whatever your style of eating, whatever be your importance amongst men, one thing is
certain, a mango eater is born special, and if they are not regarded as the chosen ones,
it is the great disservice and sheer blasphemy against the all-mighty himself, who is the
man's creator, and also that of the godly fruit- mango.
- African proverb
